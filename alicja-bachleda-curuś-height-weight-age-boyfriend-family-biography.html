    Alicja Bachleda-Curuś is a Polish actor, singer, and voice actor. She has been cast in a variety of onscreen projects which include Vis a vis, The Gateway of Europe, Ondine, Pan Tadeusz, Syzyfowe prace, Herz über Kopf, Das Blut der Templer, Summer Storm, Comme des voleurs (à l’est), Edge, Trade, Der geköpfte Hahn, Friendship!, The Day of the Siege: September Eleven 1683, Polaris, The Girl Is in Trouble, Na dobre i na zle, Breathe, The American Side, Lokatorzy, Pitbull: Tough Women, Sperling, and 7 rzeczy, których nie wiecie o facetach. Moreover, she has done voiceover work for video games like Wolfenstein: The New Order, Wolfenstein II: The New Colossus, and Wolfenstein: Youngblood.

    Education

    Alicja Bachleda went to the 5th Liceum Ogolnoksztalcace in Krakow. She completed her high school education from there.

    In 1989, Alicja got enrolled at the National Ballet Academy in Kraków. She studied there till 1998. Then, in 1998, she started taking classes at the National Academy of Music‘s vocal department in Kraków. She went there until 2000.

    Alicja polished her acting skills by taking the course at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute in New York City.

    Boyfriend / Spouse

    Alicja Bachleda has dated –

  • Piotr Woźniak-Starak (2002-2004) – According to reports, Alicja first met Polish businessperson Piotr Woźniak-Starak in 2002. They started going soon after. At that time, both of them were in the United States. While she was honing her acting skills at Lee Strasberg’s school, he was studying at the Parsons School for Design. They dated for about 2 years before ending their relationship in 2004 by mutual consent.
  • Gregory Michael (2005) – Alicja was reported to be going out with American actor Gregory Michael in 2005. They were spotted at a number of red carpet events including Assassin Premiere Party in August 2005 in Los Angeles.
  • Colin Farrell (2009-2010) – Alicja started dating Irish actor Colin Farrell in 2009. They had grown close while working in the drama movie, Ondine. In 2009, she gave birth to their son, Henry Tadeusz Farrell. In October 2010, it was reported by the leading American and British tabloids that they had broken up. It was later reported that they had separated several months ago.
  • Sebastian Karpiel-Bułecka (2014-2015) – The leading Polish tabloids were linking Alicja with the singer Sebastian Karpiel-Bułecka throughout 2014. It was reported that they were so far gone in their relationship that they were thinking about getting married. By the start of 2015, they had called quits on their relationship.
  • Sebastian Kulczyk (2016) – In February 2016, Alicja was rumored to be going out with Polish businessperson Sebastian Kulczyk, who is known to be one of the richest Poles. The source of these rumors was their public outing as they were spotted shopping and hanging out in a café.
  • Marcin Gortat (2017-2018) – In March 2017, she started going out with former professional basketball player Marcin Gortat but the duo eventually parted ways in October 2018.

    Alicja Bachleda Facts

  • She became an honorable citizen of the city of Tampico in Mexico on May 12, 2008.
  • She loves to do her own stunts. In fact, she performed a very high and dangerous jump in the final sequence of the 2007 movie Trade on her own.
  • Alicja was just 15 years old when she was signed up for her debut movie.
  • In 2004, she tried her hands at the German cinema by appearing in the comedy-drama movie, Sommersturm (Summer Storm in English).
  • Alongside F. Murray Abraham, Enrico Lo Verso, Andrea Iaia, and Jerzy Skolimowski, she co-starred as Eleanor of Austria, Queen of Poland (Polish: Eleonora Habsburżanka) in the 2012 English-language Polish and Italian historical drama film The Day of the Siege: September Eleven 1683.
  • She appeared as a desperate woman named Signe in the 2015 American thriller film The Girl Is in Trouble. Her co-stars for the project included Columbus Short, Wilmer Valderrama, and Jesse Spencer.
  • She brought to life the character of Nikki Meeker in the 2016 mystery film The American Side.
  • In 2016, she played the character of a psychologically traumatized war photographer named Baran in the multi-cultural feature film Polaris.
