    Arn Piper's Girlfriend Is Unknown Though He Has Sparked Dating Rumors

    Spanish actor Arn Piper has sparked dating rumors with Hollywood stars such as Dua Lipa and FKA Twigs. However, despite also having an alleged girlfriend in the past, Arn Piper has kept the true nature of his love life under wraps.

    06 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Spanish actor Arón Piper has sparked dating rumors with Hollywood stars such as Dua Lipa and FKA Twigs. However, despite also having an alleged girlfriend in the past, Arón Piper has kept the true nature of his love life under wraps.

    Although he was born in Berlin, Germany, Arón Piper has risen to fame for his multiple roles in popular Spanish-speaking films and television shows such as "Elite," "Fatum," and "Muted." The actor, who comes from a mixed background, has steadfastly gained an international following.

    However, after dancing the night away with Dua Lipa, kissing FKA Twigs, and sparking romance rumors with a model and fashion designer, Arón Piper has yet to confirm any speculation about who he is dating.

    Arón Piper at the Dare In Gris Dior The Grey Zone Opening on April 11, 2023, in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

    Arón Piper's Private Life Remains Out of Public Eyes

    Piper has shared glimpses into his acting and budding music careers on Instagram through 107 pictures at the time of this publication. His over 13 million fans have seen him behind the scenes and at fashion and live shows, but he has not shared any posts indicating the presence of a significant other.

    However, in 2021, he was romantically linked to a model and fashion designer, Jessica Goicoechea. Goicoechea is originally from Barcelona, Spain, and was spotted out and about with the Spanish actor. The duo was reportedly also seen kissing in a photo but have never confirmed they are or have been in a relationship.

    Jessica Goicoechea at the Harper's BAZAAR and Carolina Herrera Fun In The Sun party on June 9, 2022, in Madrid, Spain. | Source: Getty Images

    Arón Was Reportedly Spotted with Dua Lipa in 2022

    After splitting from Anwar Hadid, her long-time boyfriend, in 2021, Dua Lipa sparked rumors of a new romance when she was reportedly seen dancing with Piper, the star of "Muted," at a nightclub in Madrid, Spain.

    Lipa and Piper's chemistry during her Future Nostalgia world tour in Spain captured fans' attention and generated social media buzz. While some fans eagerly embraced the possibility of them being a couple, others did not like the situation.

    Lipa faced accusations of being in a love triangle with Piper and FKA Twigs, as Piper was later seen sharing a passionate kiss with Twigs. However, Lipa and Piper remained silent about the romance rumors surrounding their flirtatious night out.

    Dua Lipa at the 76th annual Cannes film festival on May 19, 2023, in Cannes, France. | Source: Getty Images

    Arón Piper's Appearance in FKA Twigs' Music Video Sparked Chemistry Speculation

    Twenty-four hours after his steamy dancing with Lipa, Piper's name made the rounds again after British musician Twigs posted a video of her and Piper kissing on TikTok. In the video, Twigs, who previously dated actor Robert Pattinson for three years, was wearing a bikini while Piper was topless.

    In the TikTok clip, Twigs' song "Killer" played in the background as she and Piper shared a smooch, and it was later revealed that he starred as the love interest in the track's black and white music video.

    When the video of her song was released on YouTube, one fan commented, "...this is art at its purest. I love FKA and came to love Aaron lately. I'm in love with this project, and feels so personal to me (sic)."

    Another fan shared, "I think Aron is the ONLY one who is so perfect for this character. Can't imagine someone else. He has something angelic and demonic at the same time that is so mesmerizing. Girl, you did such a great job!"

    In One Interview, Arón Talked about Losing His Privacy

    In 2021, following the release of his long-awaited debut EP "NIEVE," Piper appeared on the cover of Numéro and opened up about the whirlwind of a year he had experienced. When asked about his biggest takeaway from 2020, Piper shared, "The loss of my privacy, no doubt." And when asked what his motto was, the actor answered:

    "Take care of life, and life with take care of you."

    Although he had to deal with losing his privacy, Piper admitted that he found confidence in those closest to him, such as his friends, family, and team. And as for fame: "...You gotta learn how to deal with it," Piper said.
