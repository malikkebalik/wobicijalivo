    All of her boyfriends and exes

    Lily-Rose Depp made quite the splash while starring in the highly controversial HBO show The Idol alongside The Weeknd. But her love life has also made headlines over the years considering her romantic links to famous Hollywood hunks like Austin Butler and Timothe Chalamet.

    04 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Lily-Rose Depp made quite the splash while starring in the highly controversial HBO show “The Idol” alongside The Weeknd.

    But her love life has also made headlines over the years — considering her romantic links to famous Hollywood hunks like Austin Butler and Timothée Chalamet.

    From models to musicians and everything in between, here are all the men the daughter of Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis has romanced.

    Ash Stymest

    Lily-Rose’s first known relationship was with British model Ash Stymest.

    Rumors first swirled that they were seeing each other in October 2015 when they were seen spending time together in Paris.

    The couple was spotted several times throughout their romance, including arriving at LAX together after attending the Cannes Film Festival for the premiere of her film “The Dancer” in October 2016, according to Daily Mail.

    Things appeared to be serious for the young lovebirds when Stymest joined Lily-Rose on a family trip to Paris and bonded with her parents and her brother, Jack, in March 2016.

    However, the duo reportedly broke up in 2018 after more than two years together when she was 16 and he was 24.

    Timothée Chalamet

    “The Idol” star and Timothée Chalamet met on the set of the 2018 Netflix film “The King.”

    They then sparked dating rumors in December 2018 after being spotted on a “low-key” date at chef Marc Murphy’s Landmarc at the Time Warner Center.

    “They were both just very relaxed, enjoying a casual low-key dinner. They dressed down in flannels and were incredibly polite to all the staff,” a source told Page Six at the time.

    The following September, they were seen vacationing in Capri, Italy.

    Chalamet reflected on their romantic Italian yacht excursion, telling GQ in 2020, “I was on this boat all day with someone I really loved, and closing my eyes, I was like, indisputably, ‘That was great.’”

    But the “Call Me By Your Name” star also shared that he was “embarrassed” when paparazzi photos surfaced of them kissing.

    “Then waking up to all these pictures, and feeling embarrassed, and looking like a real nob? All pale?” he told GQ. “And then people are like, ‘This is a PR stunt.’ A PR stunt?! Do you think I’d want to look like that in front of all of you?”

    The pair also locked lips in October 2019 at Tao Downtown following a Cinema Society screening of their movie.

    Amid the media attention, their relationship seemingly fizzled and he confirmed their breakup by sharing that he was “single” in April 2020.

    Austin Butler

    Austin Butler and Lily-Rose were intertwined in a brief romance following his 2020 split from longtime partner Vanessa Hudgens.

    She and the “Elvis” star were spotted making out in London after a dinner date in August 2021.

    But the pair — who seemingly met two years prior while filming the 2016 horror-comedy “Yoga Hosers” — were not spotted together again, signaling an end to their short-lived fling.

    Yassine Stein

    Lily-Rose reportedly moved on with French rapper Yassine Stein a month after her steamy smooch with Butler.

    The “Fill The Void” singer and Stein were photographed showing PDA while grabbing groceries on a supermarket run in Los Angeles in November 2021.

    For more Page Six you love …

    It’s unclear when they ended their relationship, but fans pointed out in March 2023 that they had both unfollowed each other on social media.

    070 Shake

    Lily-Rose made her first-ever public acknowledgment of a relationship in May 2023 when she confirmed she was dating rapper 070 Shake.

    She took to her Instagram Story to post a PDA-filled photo with the “Escapism” artist.

    “4 MONTHS W MY CRUSH,” she captioned the photo.

    That same day, 070 Shake — born Danielle Balbuena — posted a close-up photo of the couple making out.

    Both have remained relatively private about their relationship since going public with their love.
