    American actress China McClain showed off her gorgeous figure in a new video she shared on her Instagram page in which she displayed her dancing skills.

    Besides her incredible acting talent and beauty, if there is another thing that fans know China McClain for, it is her love for dancing. She has never shied away from sharing videos of herself dancing on social media.

    McClain was back to her dancing best in a new post on her Instagram page that had her showing off some great moves to Pop Hunna's "Adderall (Corvette Corvette)."

    China Anne McClain attends Tyler Perry Studios' grand opening gala at Tyler Perry Studios on October 05, 2019 | Photo: Getty Images

    The actress wore a black bra top with grey palazzo pants and rocked light makeup. She also fashioned low-cut hair and looked incredibly beautiful as she danced.

    Besides her lovely dance moves, the nature of McClain's outfit meant that fans caught sight of her belly tattoo, fit abs, and impressive figure. She captioned the video:

    "had to give him a show."

    McClain's fans were awed by her beauty and impressive show of dancing talent in the video and left her a few words of admiration in the post's comment section.

    One fan said they liked her tattoo's placement, while a second fan said they were happy she had joined TikTok. A third fan said McClain killed her dance moves.

    She revealed that fans would be seeing less of her when the fourth and final season of the show returns.

    McClain was born on August 25, 1998, and comes from an artistic family. Her father, Michael McClain, is a music producer, vocalist, writer, and sound engineer, while her mother, Shontell, is a vocalist and songwriter.

    The actress and her two sisters, Sierra and Lauryn, formed a musical group called 3mcclaingirls, while her younger brother, Gabriel, also acts, sings, and dances.

    McClain has been involved in showbiz since she was three, and it was her captivating voice that helped her get into Hollywood. She went on to star in Tyler Perry's "House of Payne" before stealing the show with her sisters in the movie, "Daddy's Little Girls."

    McClain's fanbase grew to greater heights after she starred on Disney's "A.N.T. Farm." She has risen to become a superstar at just 22 years old, and one can only wonder the extent to which the actress's career can go.

    The 22-year-old stars in CW's "Black Lightning" where she plays Jennifer Pierce/Lightning, and recently made a surprise announcement on her Instagram page.

    She revealed that fans would be seeing less of her when the fourth and final season of the show returns as she was planning on leaving the DC Comics series after the fourth season before the cast knew it would be the last.

    While fans await McClain's next movie or series role, the actress, who reunited with her "House of Payne" co-stars in August after a five-year hiatus, continues to keep them entertained with her dance moves on social media.
