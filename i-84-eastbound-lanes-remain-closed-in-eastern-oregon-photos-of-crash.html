    21 Sep 2024, 00:00

    UPDATE 3: I-84 eastbound lanes are now open in eastern Oregon. OR 245 and Or 204 are also open. Travelers should expect and be prepared for winter conditions.

    -----------------------------

    UPDATE 2: I-84 eastbound lanes are now closed at Exit 216 (six miles east of Pendleton) due to the truck crash blocking all lanes near milepost 335, 11 miles West of Huntington.

    OR 245 and OR 204 (Tollgate Hwy.) are also closed (open to local traffic only), as they are not safe detours for I-84 traffic.

    Crews are working to open one lane near the crash site. The eastbound closure moved from Baker City to La Grande, and now to Exit 216 due to limited truck parking in La Grande and Baker City.

    Please stay in a community with services until the interstate opens. Do not blindly follow detours provided by GPS navigation apps as they may not show road conditions.

    Travelers should expect and be prepared for winter conditions. Continue to check TripCheck.com or call 511 / 800-977-6368 for updates. If you are outside Oregon, call 503-588-2941.

    ----------------------------

    UPDATE: I-84 eastbound remains closed in Eastern Oregon.

    I-84 eastbound lanes are now closed at La Grande Exits 265 due to the truck crash blocking all lanes near milepost 335, 11 miles West of Huntington.

    OR 245 is also closed (open to local traffic only), as it is not a safe detour for I-84 traffic. Crews are hoping to open one lane near the crash site within a couple of hours.

    Drivers are asked to stay in the community with services until the interstate opens and to not blindly follow detours provided by GPS navigation apps as they may not show road conditions.

    ----------------------------

    I-84 eastbound lanes are closed at Baker City Exits 304 and 306 due to truck crash blocking all lanes near milepost 335, 11 miles West of Huntington.

    Crews are responding. No estimate at this time as to when the route will be open.

    The westbound lanes are not impacted by this crash. Travelers should expect and be prepared for winter conditions.
