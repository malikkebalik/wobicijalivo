    Cristiano Ronaldo: YouTube sensation IShowSpeed shows off tattoo of his hero but looks nothing alike

    YouTube sensation IShowSpeed had his idol Cristiano Ronaldo tattooed on him - though the likeness is somewhat difficult to make out. Speed has dedicated his life to fanboying the Portuguese superstar and has gone viral in his attempts to meet him.

    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    YouTube sensation IShowSpeed had his idol Cristiano Ronaldo tattooed on him - though the likeness is somewhat difficult to make out.

    Speed has dedicated his life to fanboying the Portuguese superstar and has gone viral in his attempts to meet him.

    And he took his devotion to the next level by getting a tattoo of the 37-year-old on his arm - though not before the artist pranked him.

    Speed, real name Darren Watkins Jr, was left furious after the tattooist 'revealed' the finished article to him that looked nothing alike.

    It led to the social media star going ballistic and screaming over and over again: "Who is this?"

    He was streaming the tattoo job live on YouTube, where he moved his arm and the unfinished Ronaldo pic closer to the camera before saying: "Bro, who is this?"

    The tattoo artist replied as his client continued to rage: "Hey bro, I’m just f****** with you, it’s not done. We doing pranks, right?”

    He resumed but, in truth, the finished product was not much better and the video has since gone viral across YouTube and Twitter, with one fan branding the tattoo 'Ronaldo from Wish'.

    Most read in Football

    However, Speed appeared to be delighted with the end result and let loose Ronaldo's trademark 'Siiiuuu' after being shown the true conclusion.

    The teenage star kissed the tattoo before exclaiming to the camera with a message to the Al-Nassr star, saying: “You’re now a part of me for life.”

    Speed was at the Qatar World Cup in December as he tried to catch a glimpse of Ronaldo with Portugal but was left disappointed when he learned that he was on the bench against Switzerland.

    He is one of the biggest internet personalities in the world and has 15.2 million subscribers on YouTube after getting banned from Twitch in 2021.
