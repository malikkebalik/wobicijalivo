    Who is Isabel Preysler?

    Isabel Preysler is a Filipina-Spanish former model, journalist, socialite, and television host. Likely, she started her journalism career in Spain, working for a Spanish magazine named ‘Hola’.

    Later, she became a TV host and started hosting a popular show called ‘Hoy En Casa’. She rose to fame through her strong journalistic ethics and her approach to charitable deeds.
