    Summer Walker has taken a moment to troll her ex-boyfriend Lil Meech — but has insisted it’s all in good fun.

    On Wednesday (October 25), the “Girls Need Love” singer took to her Instagram Stories to share a photo of the BMF actor with her singing the lyrics to Mystikal’s “Danger (Been So Long)” in the background.

    Summer Walker and Meech appeared to start dating in May when the couple were spotted out together. However, their relationship hit a rough patch — and ultimately appeared to end for good — when Meech was allegedly caught cheating.

    Over the summer, the R&B singer ignited social media with a series of Instagram Stories seemingly accusing the BMF star of cheating on her.

    Less than 24 hours later, footage captured by a Ring doorbell emerged of Meech entering a woman’s apartment and then leaving 17 minutes later.

    With the clip spreading online, Lil Meech attempted to clear up the confusion on his Instagram Story by claiming that he was helping his cousin bring in some groceries. “Damn, I can’t help my cousin bring the bags in the house,” he asked. “We went to the grocery store, man.”
