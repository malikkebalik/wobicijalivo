    Oklahoma WR Theo Wease Jr. transferring to Missouri

    Theo Wease Jr. caught 19 passes for 378 yards and four touchdowns this year. His best season came as a sophomore in 2020, when he hauled in 37 passes for 530 yards and four touchdowns.

    Wease was a five-star recruit out of Allen (Texas) High in 2019.

    Iowa LB Jestin Jacobs commits to Oregon

    This is a big pickup for Oregon and a big loss for Iowa. Had he not suffered a season-ending biceps injury, linebacker Jestin Jacobs was a likely top-100 draft pick.

    Virginia adds a top FCS QB

    Virginia lands Monmouth transfer Tony Muskett, one of the top FCS QBs on the market.

    Muskett, a Springfield, Va., native, threw for 1,997 yards, 17 touchdowns and eight interceptions this season.

    Iowa State lands former Stanford RB Arlen Harris

    The Wentzville, Mo., native played in one game this year as a freshman, carrying two times for 1 yard. He was the No. 30 running back in the 2022 recruiting class, per the 247Sports Composite Rating.

    All-AAC CB Ja'Quan Sheppard transfers to Maryland

    Former Cincinnati cornerback Ja'Quan Sheppard committed to Maryland on Sunday.

    Sheppard emerged as a first-team All-AAC player this season, recording 50 tackles, 4 1/2 tackles for loss and 10 pass breakups.

    USC lands Georgia State transfer OLB Jamil Muhammad

    A quarterback out of high school, Muhammad originally signed with Vanderbilt before enrolling at Georgia State. He moved to linebacker and recorded six sacks as a redshirt sophomore in 2021. He had 44 tackles and 1 1/2 sacks in 2022.

    Louisville DL Caleb Banks is bound for Florida

    Banks, a three-star recruit out of high school, recorded two tackles, a sack and a forced fumble in six games this season as a redshirt freshman.

    Keagan Johnson commits to Kansas State

    Iowa transfer WR Keagan Johnson has committed to Kansas State.

    SMU adds a a pair of former Miami players

    SMU lands commitments from former Hurricanes Elijah Roberts and Jaylan Knighton in the transfer portal. Knighton was Miami’s leading rusher in 2021 when SMU coach Rhett Lashlee was offensive coordinator. Roberts is a defensive lineman.

    Corey Crooms commits to Minnesota

    Former Western Michigan WR Corey Crooms commits to Minnesota.

    Zakhari Spears leaving Washington

    Zakhari Spears, a redshirt freshman CB this season, announces he will enter the transfer portal. 2021 signee.

    Eddie Czaplicki transferring to USC

    USC continuing to add proven Pac-12 players to its roster with Arizona State transfer P Eddie Czaplicki, a first-team All-P12 selection.

    Veteran tackle Cam Wire leaving LSU

    LSU veteran tackle Cam Wire has entered the transfer portal, The Athletic has learned. Opening day starter lost the job after the Florida State game. Been a steady contributor for LSU during the past few years.

    Kent State WR Devontez Walker has entered the portal

    Kent State WR Devontez Walker has entered the transfer portal, The Athletic has learned.

    First-team All-MAC wideout who ranked No. 2 in the conference with 921 receiving yards and 11 TDs this season.

    Jimari Butler not leaving Nebraska after all

    Jimari Butler is staying at Nebraska after he entered the transfer portal Wednesday. Butler, a 6-foot-5, 245-pound edge rusher, thanked outside coaches for the opportunities offered but tweeted that after talks with his mother and the Nebraska coaches, he's not leaving.

    Butler, a sophomore next season, could fit as a key figure up front in the defense of coordinator Tony White, who has reportedly been added to Matt Rhule’s staff.

    Every offseason, the transfer portal becomes less of a boogeyman in the eyes of fans and coaches, and we continue to learn more about the player movement behaviors and trendlines in this era of college football. Comparing last year with this one, much of the transfer portal heat is tied to high-profile coaching changes, but we haven’t seen many of those so far.

    So the portal is light on easily recognizable names right now. The transfer window stays open until Jan. 18.
