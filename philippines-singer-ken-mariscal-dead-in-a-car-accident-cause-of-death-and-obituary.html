    Ken Mariscal Death: Singer Killed in Hit-and-Run Accident

    Philippino singer Ken Mariscal was involved in a tragic accident last night when a white SUV car hit him as he was walking on an empty road. After colliding with him, the car didn’t stop and ran away. The crowd near the accident gathered around Mariscal.

    However, none of them tried rushing the severely injured man to the hospital. Instead, they were seen taking pictures and recording videos. By the time, Mariscal was taken to the nearby medical facility, he was already dead.

    Doctors confirmed that Ken Mariscal died on Saturday, July 08, 2023, due to the fatal injuries sustained in the hit-and-run accident. An investigation is launched to find the culprit.

    Who was Ken Mariscal?

    Ken Mariscal was a rising singer from Davao City, Philippines. He worked with several bands as a vocalist and also performed as a freelancer at various events. Recently, Mariscal was working as an offshore representative at Salford College, Australia.

    Before this, he studied media and communication students at the Ateneo de Davao University. Later, he started working with Ant Savvy Creatives Inc. as a sales representative for Wilkins Distilled Drinking Water. He also appeared in Talent Edge and reached the grand finale.

    Mariscal spent most of his professional life working as a singer in Davao City’s music and events industries. He is best known for hosting “Kapamilya Mas Winner Ka, Davao!” and was a “Tawag ng Tanghalan” (It’s Showtime) alumnus.

    Who killed Ken Mariscal?

    The police are yet to catch the driver of the white car that hit Ken Mariscal and escaped on July 8th. An investigation was launched for the accident and the police are trying to find the culprit. He should be in custody very soon.
