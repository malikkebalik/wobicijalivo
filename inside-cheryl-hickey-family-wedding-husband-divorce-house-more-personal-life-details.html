    Cheryl Hickey's Short Bio: Age, Height, Reporting

    Born on 8 January 1976 in Owen Sound, Ontario, Cheryl, age 42, possesses the height of 5 feet 4 inches (1.63m). 

    She attended West Hill Secondary School, where she was Student Council President. At the age of 16, she was working at a local cable station. During that time, she realized that she wanted to be a reporter. 

    After that, she joined Fanshawe College in London, Ontario to study Radio and Television Arts.

    Cheryl Hickey's Career and Net Worth

    Cheryl Hickey, who has a net worth of $4 million, has graduated from the Television and Radio program at Ontario's Fanshawe College and worked for the local radio station K106.5 as a correspondent afterward followed by an afternoon news anchoring.

    She joined the Global Television Network in May 1999, where she worked as a photojournalist and as a news reporter. Due to her hard work, she became the host of Global Television Network’s program ET Canada in September 2005.
