    The actress first started her career through modeling at the age of 14. 

    After winning the Pernambucanas Faces contest in the national phase, Queiroz signed a contract with Ford Models.

    Contracted by a local agency, Queiroz moved to Japan and started living there at 16. Two years later, she moved to New York to continue modeling.

    In 2015, aged 21, she got her first acting role as Angel in a telenovela, Verdades Secretas. Queiroz’s performance was well received.

    The actress received many awards for her performance in the show, and it’s only uphill from here.
