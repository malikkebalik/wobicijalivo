    If you want to get a great body, then you need to exercise daily. Going to the gym is the best option but it may not be always possible due to time constraints and high membership fees. So, many people now consider working out at home by creating a home gym. Among the various exercise equipment available in the market, you can buy a cross trainer for a total body workout. Usually, most people have limited space in their home and it is not possible to accommodate equipment effective for each body part. So, having a cross-trainer means, your entire body will be toned using just one machine. When choosing a cross trainer, you must focus on a few factors like the build quality, price, and features. If you are looking for high quality and effective cross trainer, then you can buy the ProForm Hybrid Trainer.

    Design

    It is a combination of two machines: an elliptical and a recumbent exercise bike. The stride length is 15 inches which is wide enough for users with big feet. The handles provide soft grips and help in upper body workouts. Two stationary handgrips provide heart rate monitoring. So, you will be able to perform heart zone training. It has an LCD monitor for displaying distance, speed, calories burned, time elapsed, and other essential data. You don’t need external electricity to operate the LCD monitor.

    The flywheel provides smooth operation. It has a Watts Power Meter that tracks your workout data. It has an EKG Grip Pulse for monitoring heart rate. The SMR Silent Magnetic Resistance allows frictionless resistance. It is iPod compatible. There is a media holder, so you will be able to watch a movie or listen to songs while exercising. There is an onboard sound system as well, so you can listen to music through the 2-inch dual speakers. There is a water bottle holder as well, so you will be able to stay hydrated always.

    Build Quality

    It has a sturdy steel frame construction that makes the cross-trainer durable. It is stable, so you can work out with confidence. The pedals are ideal for people with larger feet. The pedals are textured as well and so your feet won’t slip. The stationary handlebars have a heart monitoring system to record your heart rate. The handlebars also provide soft grips so that you can work out for long hours. You get a 5-year guarantee for the frame.

    It has lots of extra features that you won’t find in the other cross-trainers like a bottle holder, media holder, and more. It provides a quiet and smooth operation, so you will be able to work out for longer hours. It is built in such a way that it allows low impact exercise; so, if you are suffering from an injury, you will find comfort by working out with this machine.

    Size

    This equipment is 70.5 inches long, 24.5 inches wide, and 60.5 inches high. It weighs 116.8 pounds, which is quite heavy. The good news is that there are transport wheels for easy relocation.

    Comfort

    It has an adjustable seat that makes workouts easy. You can easily switch from using the elliptical machine to the recumbent bike. The handles provide soft grips. The pedals are textured that keeps feet secure. The silent magnetic technology ensures that you will be able to workout without producing any noise.

    Ease of Use

    You will be able to assemble the equipment easily as an instruction manual with fully illustrated pictures is included. It takes about 1 hour to assemble. 

    Effectiveness

    With this equipment, you get access to lots of workouts. All you need to do is connect your tablet using Bluetooth. You get access to iFit and a library of on-demand video workouts that you can tailor it to your fitness level and goals. The ifit trainers will be able to control the speed and incline of your machine using Bluetooth technology in real-time. So, you can get the most out of your exercise routine.

    Using this machine, you will be able to stand up for a full-body elliptical workout our lean back to the cycle. It is great for cardio training and weight loss. With 16 different workout programs, you will be able to reach your fitness goals easily. Eight exercises are designed for speed training and the other 8 for burning calories. It is iFit enabled; as a result, you will be able to access plenty of workouts using your mobile device. Every day you will be able to download a personalized work out for yourself. You get immersive Google Maps workouts with Street View as well to make your workout sessions more interesting and enjoyable. You can also take part in online bike races. This will keep you motivated to reach new fitness goals.

    Pros

    • It has transport wheels that make it easy to move around.
    • The pedals are oversized and adjustable, so it is appropriate for users of different heights.
    • It has a water bottle holder, so you can stay hydrated during exercise.
    • It has a large LCD display which makes it easy to read the workout data like distance, time, calories burned, etc.  
    • The monitor operates with a battery; no electricity is needed.
    • It has a maximum weight limit of 350 lbs, which makes it ideal for heavy users as well.
    • It provides 16 different resistance levels.
    • 16 training workout apps will help to burn calories and improve your performance.
    • The Watts Power Meter shows when you should speed up or slow down.
    • It has an adjustable seat that allows you to work out comfortably.
    • It is a Smartphone compatible.
    • You get a 5-year warranty on the frame and 90-day warranty on the parts and labor.

    Cons

    • There is no cooling fan.
    • The flywheel is light, so stability can be an issue.
    • It cannot be folded.

    Final Verdict

    The company has already earned a good name for manufacturing high quality and user-friendly exercise equipment. This cross trainer is budget-friendly equipment that can help you to achieve your fitness goals. It is a combination of an elliptical and a recumbent bike. You get a variety of exercise options with it. You get 16 workout apps which will help you to perform different exercises. You will be able to vary the intensity of the exercises as well to burn more calories within a short time. So, this cross trainer is ideal if you are serious about getting a fit body.
